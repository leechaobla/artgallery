About Us

Our Story

At InArt, we believe in the transformative power of art. Our platform is a dedicated space for artists and art lovers to share, discover, and celebrate creativity. Whether you're a seasoned artist or just starting out, InArt provides the tools and community you need to showcase your work and connect with others.

Our Mission

InArt was founded in 2024 by a group of art enthusiasts who recognized the need for a more inclusive and accessible online art community. We saw the potential for technology to bring artists together from around the world, allowing them to share their work and gain exposure in a supportive environment. Our goal has always been to democratize the art world and create a space where all voices can be heard.
